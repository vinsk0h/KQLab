# KQLab — Logo Design Philosophy
## Movement: "Forensic Syntax"

### Form & Space
The mark occupies a hexagonal frame — geometry borrowed from molecular structure and honeycomb logic, signaling a laboratory of systematic intelligence. Six equal sides suggest order, precision, and the recursive nature of query-based investigation. The hexagon sits within a dark rectangular canvas, its edges hovering just inside the boundary, creating tension between containment and expansiveness. Nothing is wasted. Every millimeter earns its place.

### Color & Material
Three tones govern the mark with the discipline of a master colorist. Near-black (`#09090b`) grounds the canvas — the void of an unlit terminal. Crimson (`#e63946`) traces the hexagonal frame and the chevron operator, a color that in security contexts carries the weight of alert and authority. Cyan (`#00d4ff`) strikes the vertical cursor — cold, precise, the color of live data streams and network topology maps. This tricolor system was calibrated with painstaking attention: each hue chosen not for decoration but for semantic weight.

### Rhythm & Symbol
Two marks inhabit the interior with the economy of a minimalist master. The chevron — `>` — is the KQL query prefix, the universal operator of forward motion and conditional logic. The vertical bar — `|` — is the cursor, the pipe operator, the pause between computation and result. Together they read as a terminal prompt frozen mid-thought: `> |`. This is the mark of someone who works in the space between question and answer. The composition was refined through countless iterations, each stroke positioned with surgical exactness.

### Hierarchy & Adaptability
The mark functions across every scale from 16×16 pixel favicon to full-resolution display with zero visual degradation. At the smallest size, the hexagon reads as a geometric shield; the interior marks remain legible as pure abstraction. At larger sizes, the semantic content — the KQL syntax — emerges. This scalar intelligence is the product of deep expertise in systematic mark-making, where every node was designed to communicate its essential meaning regardless of context or medium.

### Craft & Intent
This is not decoration applied to a product. This is a compressed argument about what the tool does: it holds query logic (the hexagon as container), executes it (the `>` operator), and awaits result (the `|` cursor). The mark functions as a diagram of the discipline itself. It was labored over with the care of someone who understands that a logo seen ten thousand times must reward each viewing — that the smallest detail, the precise weight of a stroke, the exact coordinates of a vertex, compound into the difference between a mark that commands respect and one that merely occupies space.
